Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions about admission, routines, hostel facilities, and recruitment.

What is the admission procedure for new students?

Parents or guardians can apply online by clicking "Apply Now" on our homepage. Complete the Admission Form, upload the required documents (Aadhaar Card, Birth Certificate, and Photos), and submit the application. Once reviewed and approved by the administrator, you will be assigned a class, roll number, and a unique registration code.

What are the class timings and school routing?

Bhogram Al-Hidayet Mission runs classes from Nursery up to Class VI. Normal school hours are Monday to Thursday and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 11:30 AM, and Friday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM. Coaching Classes start from {{ coaching_class_time }} on every class days. Detailed daily schedules and routines are posted on the digital notice boards and are also viewable inside the Student and Teacher dashboards.

Is there a hostel facility at this branch?

No, we do not offer residential hostel facilities at this branch right now. Only day hostel facilities are available, which include dedicated study hours, learning support, and nutritious meals for day hostel students.

How do I pay school and hostel fees?

Online payments are currently disabled and will start soon. For now, payments should be made directly in cash or via bank transfer at the school's administration office. Official payment receipts will be generated and handed over instantly.

How can qualified teachers join Al-Hidayet Mission?

We are always looking for passionate, experienced educators to join our team. You can apply directly through our "Application Portal" under the "Teacher Joining Request" tab. Fill in your academic details, Aadhaar card number, qualifications, and preferred classes/subjects.
