Services & Facilities

Comprehensive educational and residential facilities designed to nurture every student's potential.

Computer Education

State-of-the-art computer labs with high-speed internet and modern software. We emphasize digital literacy from an early age, covering programming basics to office applications.

Hostel Facility

Secure and separate hostel buildings for Boys and Girls. Our wardens ensure a disciplined yet homely environment with study hours, regular meals, and 24/7 supervision.

Transport Service

Reliable bus services covering major routes including Mastanpara and surrounding areas. Our vehicles are well-maintained and staffed with attendants for student safety.

Psychological Care

We believe in mental well-being alongside academic success. Regular counseling sessions help students manage stress, build confidence, and maintain a healthy mindset.

Special Coaching

Extra coaching support is available for students needing additional help. Our dedicated teachers provide personalized attention to ensure every concept is clearly understood.

Digital Classrooms

Modern classrooms equipped with audio-visual aids to make learning interactive and engaging. We integrate technology into daily lessons for better retention.
